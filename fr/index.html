delta group

The Delta Group is a leading global supplier of new, refurbished and used heavy machinery for general construction, infrastructure, road building, mineral extraction projects and machinery related to the oil & gas industry.

From single machines to complete fleets, the Delta Group is able to meet all your heavy machinery requirements, including service, maintenance, spare- and wear parts, training, and consultancy.

Our stock of over 1,000 units is sourced from a worldwide network and where necessary, we refurbish used machinery to meet our customer’s needs, using our own workshops and skilled personnel.

innovative padding and screening solutions

The XROK Granite 400 is a modern, innovative and compact tracked padding machine with a uniquely designed, patent pending, high energy two deck scalping screen-box. The total screen area of 11.52m2 has the option of a top deck finger bar configuration, giving greater segregation of padding/ backfill material resulting in increased production, reduced clogging and limits impact damage to the bottom deck.
