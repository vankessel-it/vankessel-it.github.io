delta workshop

Our fully equipped (9500 m2) workshop and sandblasting/ painting facility is located in Deurne (the Netherlands), which is well situated for both Rotterdam and Antwerp Ports. Refurbishment, inspection, testing, rebuilding and painting is carried out at our workshop in Deurne. We have a team of professional engineers and mechanics with in-house facilities for repair and maintenance as well as full refurbishment, meaning we are not reliant on outside services or suppliers to meet deadlines.

Our in-house capabilities give us total control over quality and we do not rely on outside services or suppliers to meet deadlines. More than just paint, Delta add’s value.

We carry out internal inspections to our own very high standards as well as work with independent inspection organizations such as TÜV, Veritas and SGS. If required, our heavy machinery can undergo a Caterpillar TA inspection or similar inspections from other leading equipment manufacturers.
