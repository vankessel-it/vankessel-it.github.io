delta workshop

Our fully equipped (9500 m2) workshop and sandblasting/ painting facility is located in Deurne (the Netherlands), which is well situated for both Rotterdam and Antwerp Ports. Refurbishment, inspection, testing, rebuilding and painting is carried out at our workshop in Deurne. We carry out internal inspections to our own very high standards as well as work with independent inspection organizations such as TÜV, Veritas and SGS. If required, our heavy machinery can undergo a Caterpillar TA inspection or similar inspections from other leading equipment manufacturers.

Our state-of-the-art 9,500 square meter fully equipped workshop and sandblasting/painting facility is located in Deurne, The Netherlands which is well situated for both Rotterdam and Antwerp Ports. Our workshop has a team of professional engineers and skilled mechanics with in-house facilities for inspections, testing, maintenance, service, repairs, refurbishment, rebuilding, welding, fabrication and is fully equipped for sandblasting & painting. This gives us the capability to bring used heavy machinery up to the standard you require, and is reassurance that it is reliable and able to do the job it is intended for.
