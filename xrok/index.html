XROK

XROK is a manufacturer of innovative padding and screening machinery as well as wheeled and tracked stockpiling conveyors. Delta Pipeline Machinery B.V. is the exclusive distributor for sale and rental of XROK equipment.

View XROK stock

XROK Granite 400 Double Deck Pipeline Padding Machine

The XROK Granite 400 is a modern, innovative and compact tracked padding machine with a uniquely designed, patent pending, high energy two deck scalping screen-box. The total screen area of 11.52 M2 has the option of a top deck finger bar configuration, giving greater segregation of padding/ backfill material resulting in increased production, reduced clogging and limits impact damage to the bottom deck.

Using many years’ experience earned in the quarry & screening industry and with the customer’s requirements in mind the unique design and configuration of the double deck screen-box gives the ability to process large or small, abrasive or cohesive, material in both dry and wet sticky conditions at high volumes which reduces the cost per M3.

For sales and/or rental please contact Delta Pipeline Machinery B.V.
