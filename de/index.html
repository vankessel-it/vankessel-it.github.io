Delta Group

The Delta Group is a global supplier of new, refurbished and used heavy machinery for infrastructure construction, mineral extraction projects, quarry, mining and machinery related to the pipeline industry. From single machines to complete fleets, the Delta Group is able to meet all your heavy machinery requirements, including service, maintenance, spare- and wear parts, training, and consultancy.

The XROK Granite 400 is a modern, innovative and compact tracked padding machine with a uniquely designed, patent pending, high energy two deck scalping screen-box. The total screen area of 11.52m2 has the option of a top deck finger bar configuration, giving greater segregation of padding/ backfill material resulting in increased production, reduced clogging and limits impact damage to the bottom deck.
